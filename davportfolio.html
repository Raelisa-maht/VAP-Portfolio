DAV - Sales analysis by product dashboard

Project Background

Northwind Traders imports and exports speciality food around the world. It is looking at expanding its business. Before deciding on their expansion plan, their management would like to understand more about their current sales analyse in terms of products, employees, suppliers, customers, and inventory. Your team has been tasked to develop reports to show your analysis and explain your approach to the expansion.

My role and solution

My role in this project was to develop the POWER BI Sales Analysis by Product dashboard. I took on the task of designing and implementing the reporting solution to provide insights into our sales performance by product. This involved gathering requirements from stakeholders, preparing and modeling the data, designing the dashboard layout, implementing features for interactivity, and ensuring the accuracy and usability of the dashboard.

My process

Requirement Gathering and Analysis

Understand the objectives and requirements for the dashboard. Identify key metrics and desired visualizations to drive insights into sales analysis by product.

Data Preparation and Modeling

Cleanse and transform raw data to create a structured data model optimized for analysis in Power BI. Define relationships between tables, create calculated columns and measures, and optimize query performance.

Dashboard Design and Development

Design the dashboard layout using Power BI Desktop, selecting appropriate visualizations to present sales analysis by product effectively. Utilize a variety of chart types and interactive features to enhance user experience.

Testing and Validation

Conduct thorough testing to validate the accuracy and performance of the dashboard. Cross-reference dashboard outputs with source data and conduct user acceptance testing with stakeholders to ensure alignment with requirements.

Deployment, Maintenance, and Iteration

Deploy the dashboard to the Power BI Service for broader access within the organization. Establish refresh schedules to keep the data up-to-date. Continuously monitor dashboard usage and performance, proactively addressing any issues or enhancement opportunities. Iterate on the dashboard based on feedback and evolving business needs.
